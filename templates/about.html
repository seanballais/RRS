    The Department Of Computer Science Room Reservation System

    The DCS Room Reservation System is an online system designed to make room reservation in the department easier and more streamlined.

    Faculty and staff of the department may reserve slots for their classes and events. Administrators can manage classes and requests from the public to reserve rooms.
